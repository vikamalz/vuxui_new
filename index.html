About Me.

I have experience in designing innovative and user-centred solutions for complex systems. I bring a strong technical awareness and a passion for creative problem solving to each product.

My key areas of expertise include:

  • Building atomic design systems from concept to implementation and guiding product teams in their usage.
  • Conducting user research to develop personas, user journeys and more.
  • Designing digital projects from information architecture to wireframes and user testings.
  • Creating prototypes and supporting technical implementation.

Based in Hannover, Germany, I am currently a working student at MHP - A Porsche Company in the Industrial Cloud Solutions cluster. I am doing my Bachelor's degree at the Faculty of Design, specialising in Digital Environments, at the HAWK University of Applied Sciences and Arts. Some of my work, completed during university projects is showcased below.
